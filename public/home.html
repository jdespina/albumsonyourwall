Albums on Your Wall

How this works

Search an artist name, press "GO" and it will take you to their albums page. By clicking on an album, it will take you to a customizable image, which you are more than welcome to download or screenshot. For more information scroll down!

Download Pictures

Directions

Once you enter the album's page, feel free to change the color of the images. By clicking on the track title, it will send you straight to its spotify link.
Once you are happy with the colors, just click the "Save Photo" button. Keep in mind these pictures are large. This will automatically download the image to your download folder. You can use this image as a background for your phone or even print it out. Enjoy :))

Here are some resources:

  • FreePrintsNow.com is a really good app to print out these photos which will be sent to your home. All you have to pay for is shipping.
  • Shortrunposters.com has cheap 18"x24" posters you can print and get sent straight to your house.
  • You could always save these photos to a flash drive and take it to your closest poster printing place such as FedEx, Office Max, or Staple and print it out.
  • The images this website provides look best in matte.
